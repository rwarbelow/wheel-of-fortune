Welcome to Wheel of Fortune

To play:

  1. Click the wheel.
  2. Choose a consonant (yellow letters).
  3. If the letter appears in the puzzle, you earn money!
  4. Once you've earned enough money, you can start buying vowels (orange letters). Each vowel costs $250 for every time it appears in the puzzle.
  5. If the spinner lands on the red "BANKRUPT" slice, you must start over.
  6. At any time, you can click "I would like to solve" if you know what the puzzle says.
